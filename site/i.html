What is MALT?

Chat in any language without the need to jump back and forth from a translation tool.

With MALT's instant and automatic language translation, it is the perfect tool for global businesses, language students or anyone who wants to converse with someone in their native language without the hassle of using a separate translation software.

What makes MALT different?

Translating messages can be difficult and time consuming. While a variety of software exists that will translate your messages, they require multiple steps and are not natively integrated into your messaging apps. MALT requires no difficult set up, just connect with anyone in the world and your messages will be translated instantly once sent.

Automatic translation

No need to copy or paste text into a translator, your message will automatically translate into each user's selected language.
