What is Comprendo?

Connect with anyone in any language instantly.

With Comprendo's instant and automatic language translation, it is the perfect tool for global businesses, language students or anyone who wants to converse with someone in their native language without the hassle of using a separate translation software.

What Makes Comprendo Different?

Comprendo eliminates the need for a translation tool

Translating messages can be difficult and time consuming. While a variety of software exists that will translate your messages, they require multiple steps and are not natively integrated into your messaging apps. Connect with anyone in the world and your messages will be translated instantly once sent.

Automatic translation

No need to copy or paste text into a translator, your message will automatically translate into each user's selected language.

108 languages supported

Comprendo uses Google Translate, the most accurate translation engine available.

Cross-platform compatible

Comprendo works in any web browser, desktop or mobile.

New features added regularly

New features are added regularly, making Comprendo your go to messaging application for global communication.
